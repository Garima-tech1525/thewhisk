The Restaurant

The Whisk began on April 26th, 2002 in the city of Ferozepur. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and Tradition” the company is family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch. The Whisk has three generations of family member working together to fulfill the idea “Honor the Customer, Quality Comes First”. Through hard work and dedication, The Whisk began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic traditional Indian food.
